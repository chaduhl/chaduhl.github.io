Hi, I'm Chad. I study Roman religion & Latin literature

with a sprinkling of computational text analysis.

I'm currently finishing my master's degree in Classics, working on domestic Roman religion and literature in the first century CE, natural language processing (NLP) in Latin and Greek, and developing a new site for the collections at the Wilcox Classical Museum.

My thesis focuses on the changing attitudes to the lares and the penates in the first century CE. There has been a significant dearth of scholarship on this facet of Roman religion since most publications narrow their focus on the Republican Period and the Augustan Age. I aim to augment our understanding of how literature from the Neronian and Flavian periods employ domestic religion to participate in public discourses. This will help us to better understand how the cultural values surrounding the lares and the penates and how their perceptions may have shifted after Augustus' major religious reforms.
